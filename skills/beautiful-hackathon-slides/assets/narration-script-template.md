## Notes for Recording

- **Tone**: Confident, conversational. Not overly formal. Think "smart friend explaining a cool project."
- **Pace**: Most AI TTS sounds best at ~170 wpm. Adjust if your chosen tool runs fast or slow.
- **Emphasis words**: Mark words with italics that should be stressed. Good emphasis words communicate product differentiation (*actually*, *real*, *live*, *yours*).
- **Pauses**: Em dashes (—) and ellipses (...) tell the AI voice to take a breath. Use them to avoid run-on delivery.
- **Recommended AI voice tools**:
  - **ElevenLabs** — Most natural, paid, best for final production
  - **OpenAI TTS** — Solid quality, voices: alloy / nova / onyx / echo
  - **PlayHT** — Good for conversational tone
  - **Mac system voice** — Free fallback
